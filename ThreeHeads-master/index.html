FAQ

You have questions, we have answers

Do I need to be home?

Just like food delivery, you need to be home for pickups and deliveries - unless you want to leave your order with your door attendant. But we're speedy, all requests are fulfilled promptly..

When are my clothes ready?

Typically orders are clean in 24-36 hours. Once clean, you're notified via SMS that your order is ready along with the next available delivery time. We also email you a line-itemed receipt for your records.

What are your hours?

We make sure we have cleaners available 7 days a week, including nights and weekends. Our goal is to make dry cleaning quick and simple.
