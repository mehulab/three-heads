The environment is important to us

Environmentally Conscious Cleaning

The environment is important to Three Heads, so it’s fundamental that our partners utilize eco-friendly laundry practices including water and resource conservation and peroxide bleach instead of chlorine bleach. In addition to being better for our Earth, peroxide bleach results in less linting and longer life for your garments. We look for partners that have equipment that includes continuous batch washers which use minimal amounts of water compared to standard washers. Water can be monitored and treated for proper pH prior to discharge. Cleaners can also stage all work to take advantage of minimum energy use; this includes placing the boiler on low fire whenever possible.
