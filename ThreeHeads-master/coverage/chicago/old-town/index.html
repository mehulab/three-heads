Dry Cleaning and Laundry Delivery in Old Town

Old Town in Chicago is a relatively affluent and historic neighborhood located north of Division Street, west of Lararbee Street and south of the former Ogden Avenue right of way. The neighborhood includes parts of Lincoln Park and the near Northside. Old Town is one of Chicago’s most famous neighborhoods as it is one of the few areas of Chicago that has buildings that withstood the Chicago Fire in 1871.
